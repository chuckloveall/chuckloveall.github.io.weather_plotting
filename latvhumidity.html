Latitude v Humidity

There is no overall trend in Humidity with latitude, however there are clusters of note. The cluster between (-20,40) has a large portion of the lower humidities. In my sample of cities, there were a number of South American cities and Saharan Africa, perhaps skewing these latitudes towards non-humid results. There is another cluster of note in the (60,80) range. Perhaps cities in these extreme northern areas are all situated close to mountains or the Polar Front, causing 0 humidity.
